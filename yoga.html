Designing A One Page Website For A Small Business.

I am often asked to create simple websites for people who are self-employed. The crucial thing with these projects is to convey a sense of personality, as well as clarity about what the small business is offering.

For my yoga website, I gained a lot of useful feedback over social media. I'm pretty happy with the finished result.

I used: pencil and paper, Photoshop, HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, Formspree.

The above was one of my shortlist logo designs for my business, Lucy Rose Yoga. I created it following a trip to India, and was keen to incorporate all of the colours of the energy body. However, people I spoke to in the UK felt that the geometrical form and colour scheme appeared mystic or esoteric. This concerned me, because my aim as a Yoga Instructor is to make yoga accessible to everyone. So I opted for a fresher look that still uses a powerful geometric form: the vesica piscis.
