A Stylish One-Page Website.

Simple business websites need to convey a sense of personality, as well as clarity about what the company or individual does.

For my personal yoga website, I gained a lot of useful feedback over social media. I'm pretty happy with the finished result.

The above was one of my shortlist logo designs for LucyRoseYoga. I created it following a trip to India, and was keen to incorporate all of the colours of the energy body. However, people I spoke to in the UK felt that the geometrical form and colour scheme combined appeared mystic or esoteric. This concerned me, because my aim as a Yoga Instructor is to make yoga accessible to everyone. So I opted for a fresher look that still uses a powerful geometric form in the vesica piscis. The colours are calmer and evocative of nature, so the message is more down to earth and inviting I hope!

I used: pencil and paper, Photoshop, HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, Formspree.
