UX Research For An Advanced Grammar Revision App.

Te'udat Bagrut (Hebrew: תעודת בגרות‎) is the high school matriculation certificate in Israel. I researched how to approach building an advanced grammar app that would help students to revise for the exam in short bursts. This was a project in collaboration with Founders and Coders, Nazareth.

Here is my comprehensive UX research document on GitHub, with user profiling, market research, pedagogical analysis, basic prototyping, colour theory in the learning environment, and typographical suggestions.
