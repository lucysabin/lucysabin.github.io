StopMyCraving: UX design for an e-commerce food tech startup.

Client

StopMyCraving is a healthy snacks company, judged a UK Top 20 startup last year, with a place on Europe's Number 1 accelerator programme this year.

Brief

I have been working closely with the developer, CEO, and nutritionist to improve the user journey since October 2017.

Deliverables

Documented insights into customer behaviour, re-design of entire e-commerce platform (under construction), design of new "snack boxes" to suit user needs, and recyclable packaging with branding (pictured).

Process

StopMyCraving are growing rapidly. Each design phase is as efficient as possible. I conduct user testing with targeted groups to measure key variables that impact sales figures. Because of the business aims, a lot of my work has revolved around investigating customer behaviour when they are hungry or experiencing specific cravings.

Outcomes

Since I joined StopMyCraving in October as a freelancer, they have been accepted onto a major accelerator programme in Europe. The new products and packaging I've created are now retailing on Danish department store, Moodings.

I'm using: Skype (user interviews), pencil and paper for rapid prototyping, Sketch app, Sketch up (3D modelling), Photoshop, Illustrator, inVision, Zeplin.
