StopMyCraving: User journeys, E-commerce Platform, Packaging, Branding.

Not yet live! StopMyCraving is a healthy snacks company, judged a UK Top 20 startup last year, with a place on Europe's Number 1 accelerator programme this year.

As their lead designer, I have been working with the team to develop user journeys, redesign the e-commerce platform, design new products, and align the confident brand identity with snazzy recyclable packaging (pictured above on a partner's online store).

I wish I could share the visuals with you now; fortunately it won't be long until the new e-commerce platform is up!

I'm using: pencil and paper for rapid prototyping, Sketch, Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator.
