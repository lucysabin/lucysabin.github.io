Coot Freight: website & copy.

Client

Coot Freight Ltd. is a bespoke, international freight forwarding company.

Brief

Redesign their website and refresh the copy to reflect the businesses' identity.

Deliverables

UX report, website wireframes and copy for all sections of the website.

Process

The UX report was composed of realising user personas, in-person testing of the current website and competitors' websites, and synthesising key insights.

We learned that users appreciate bold branding, no stock images, clear and concise information regarding shipping specs, and direct contact with industry experts.

Visuals are coming soon as the website is under construction.

I'm using: pencil and paper for rapid prototyping, Sketch, Photoshop.
