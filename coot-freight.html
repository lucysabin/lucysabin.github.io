Coot Freight: Website & Copy.

Coot Freight is a bespoke, international freight forwarding company. We are working together to re-design their website and freshen up the copy.

I have conducted preliminary user testing with their current website, and websites for other shipping companies. The discoveries I made about user behaviours in relation to the industry were integral to prototyping solutions. The finalised solution is currently in the pipeline...

I'm using: pencil and paper for rapid prototyping, Sketch, Photoshop.
