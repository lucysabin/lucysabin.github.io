Promoting Diversity Within The Israeli Tech Scene.

Arab Innovators is a platform that I designed, given a brief by the British Embassy to create an online directory for startups run by Arabs in Israel.

Just 2% of startups in Israel are run by Arabs, despite the fact that they represent 20% of the population. From my research, I deduced that this is due to a range of factors; discrimination, cultural norms, and geography all play a part.

Arab Innovators aims to increase the visibility of Arab entrepreneurs in the eyes of investors, with an Events and News page that champion success stories.

The aim is to create a sense of community, while showcasing talented role models. In further iterations I would like to supplement this with a newsfeed on the landing page and more of a social media presence.

At the moment, the team and product owner are still adding to their initial database of startups and collecting feedback from investors.

As you can see, I was initially inspired by photographs of Nazareth at night, showcasing a town with a dense Arab/Palestinian population as an almost magical place full of opportunity.

I used: pencil and paper, Sketch App, InVision
