Hi, I'm a freelance UX/UI designer, specialising in websites and web apps.

My approach is

  • User-centred

    • Every stage in the design process is informed by the needs or wants of the customer/"user"/human who interacts with your product.

  • Empirical and agile

    • I do not make assumptions about how the user sees or experiences the world; I hypothesise and I investigate. I strongly believe in the value of obtaining feedback and making observations via testing, then having the agility to incorporate learnings as efficiently and effectively as possible, within the iterative design process.

  • Beautifully intuitive

    • A self-taught coder and artist at heart, I am able to respond intelligently and sensitively to both technological and aesthetic requirements.

  • Holistic and empathetic

    • I think about design in terms of crafting a dynamic experience, rather than a static thing, syncing with the user's lifestyle, thoughts, feelings, and sensations. My ultimate aim is to help create something that seamlessly resonates with their identity, and the way they perceive or experience the world, perhaps sparking a moment of joy.
